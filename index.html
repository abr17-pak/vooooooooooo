My Goal
I want to make sure every student feels confident and motivated. As House Captain, I will create a supportive and inclusive environment, encourage participation in sports and activities, and help every student step forward with pride. Leadership is about bringing out the best in everyone while fostering teamwork and energy.
Q1: How will you motivate shy students?
I will encourage them privately first, praise small achievements, and gradually involve them in group activities to boost confidence.
Q2: How will you handle conflicts in the house?
I will listen to both sides calmly, mediate fairly, and find solutions that respect everyone’s perspective.
Q3: What are your main priorities as Captain?
Ensuring team spirit, fair participation in events, boosting student confidence, and representing house interests responsibly.
